Segmented Least Squares Micro Challenge

This is a small application to help you generate data points and visualise line segments for the micro challenge. Good luck!
Click on the graph to generate points and copy the data points from the box on the right to your .makedots file.
To visualise lines copy and paste the contents of your .makedots-segments file and click generate lines.
